Trade Cancel Procedure (sample — replace with your own SOP)

Trigger: counterparty default flagged before T+1 settlement.

Step 1: Freeze the settlement queue for the affected ISIN.
Step 2: Notify the risk desk on the dedicated escalation line.
Step 3: Obtain dual approval from CRO and CFO (written, time-stamped).
Step 4: Reverse the trade in NDS-OM with reason code R-15.
Step 5: File the regulatory incident report within 60 minutes.
Step 6: Notify counterparty in writing within 2 business hours.

If any step is skipped or out of order, treat as a Sev-1 incident
and escalate per the IRP runbook.
